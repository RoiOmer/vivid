Vivid Records

Founded by Roi Omer in 2025, Vivid Records is dedicated to curating and releasing cutting-edge electronic music that blends cinematic atmospheres, organic textures, and acoustic elements. Our sound explores the balance between deep hypnotic grooves and emotional storytelling, transforming dancefloors and listening spaces into vivid sonic journeys. We welcome artists who push boundaries and craft experiences that feel alive, dynamic, and emotionally charged. From ambient intros to driving rhythms, Vivid Records stands for sound that moves both the body and the soul.
