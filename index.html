Polyoreo Records is a forward-thinking electronic music label founded by Roi Omer in 2025, focused on curating deep, cinematic sonic experiences that blend analog warmth with digital clarity. Our mission is to release tracks that tell stories — compositions that move beyond functionality and speak to emotion, space, and energy.

The label embraces a wide spectrum of electronic genres rooted in ambient, house, and experimental techno — all infused with organic textures, acoustic instrumentation, and film-inspired atmospheres. Each release is handpicked to deliver timeless audio for immersive listening environments and powerful club moments alike.

We support producers who craft detailed sound worlds, where every element—from field recordings to modular synthesis—serves a purpose. Whether it’s a late-night introspective piece or a peak-time track designed to shift reality, we champion music that vibrates with intention.

Polyoreo Records is more than a label—it's a creative sanctuary for artists and listeners who value music as art and experience. We are building a sonic archive meant to resonate, evolve, and leave a lasting imprint.
